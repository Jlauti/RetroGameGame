## Objective

Finish the enemy movement/orientation runtime so Nebula enemies behave as world-space attackers instead of player-relative lane ornaments.

Required outcomes:
- enemy locomotion must be grounded in world/terrain flow rather than directly tracking player `W`/`S` throttle changes
- enemies must visually face the player when they are telegraphing and firing, not just on spawn
- enemies must not idle at the sides in a way that reads like hovering traffic matched to player pace
- the fix must preserve the improved incoming-threat read, hostile-fire loop, and current enemy role separation
