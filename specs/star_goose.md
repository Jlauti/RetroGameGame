# Star Goose (Inspired by Star Goose, 1988)

## Aesthetic Goal
- **Era**: 1980s (DOS Age)
- **Palette**: EGA 16-color
- **Visuals**: Vertical scrolling with pseudo-3D tunnel sequences.

## Modernized Mechanics
- **Controls**: Analog movement support (modern controllers) while maintaining the arcade feel.
- **VFX**: Enhanced explosion effects that stay within the EGA palette.
- **Feedback**: Better visual/audio feedback for resource consumption (fuel/ammo).

## Core Loop
- **Vertical Shooter** over alien terrain.
- **Crystals**: Collect 48 crystals across 8 levels.
- **Resources**: Manage shields, ammunition, and fuel.
- **Terrain**: Contours that affect bullet trajectory.
- **Tunnels**: Switch to 3D perspective to resupply resources.
