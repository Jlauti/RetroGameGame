# Ice Blitz (Inspired by NHL 98, 1997)

## Aesthetic Goal
- **Era**: 1990s (Golden Age)
- **Palette**: SVGA / 16-bit
- **Visuals**: Prerendered sprites or simple 3D models with pixelated textures.
- **Audio**: High-energy chiptune/MIDI.

## Modernized Mechanics
- **AI**: Modern sports AI for teammate positioning and goalie behavior.
- **Responsiveness**: Instant switching between players, tight controls.
- **Physics**: Procedural puck physics and collisions.

## Core Loop
- **Top-down hockey** arcade action.
- **Team Play**: Fast skating, passing, and shooting.
- **Special Moves**: Slap shots, one-timers, body checks.
- **Game Structure**: Periods, scoring, and simple tournament mode.
