# Depths of Doom (Inspired by ADOM, 1994)

## Aesthetic Goal
- **Era**: 1990s (Golden Age)
- **Palette**: SVGA / 16-bit
- **Visuals**: ASCII-inspired tiles with modern lighting/shadow effects.

## Modernized Mechanics
- **Interface**: Modern inventory management (drag & drop, clear tooltips).
- **Controls**: Mouse-friendly interaction alongside classic keyboard shortcuts.
- **Save System**: Robust save state management (though maintaining permadeath challenge).
- **Auto-mapping**: High-quality auto-map feature.

## Core Loop
- **Turn-based roguelike** RPG.
- **World**: Procedurally generated dungeons with multiple branches.
- **Progression**: Deep character stats and classes.
- **Inventory**: Extensive equipment, potions, and scrolls.
- **Permadeath**: High consequences for failure.
